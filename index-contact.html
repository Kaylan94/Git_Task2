Techrown Solutions (Pty)Ltd



Techrown Solutions is a privately owned South African company that is managed by a diverse and skilled team that comprehends the importance of building strong business relationships in order to realize long-term success. Techrown endeavors to inspire generations while embracing excellence, integrity and innovation. Techrown prides itself on its invigorating, hard-working culture and industry competitiveness.

Founded in 2019, Techrown, a pioneering technology company that specializes in numerous vertical markets such as; IT, Consumer Electronics, Digital Merchandise, Mobility & Mobility Repairs (Android and Apple) as well as other business peripheral devices and consumables. The company offers and supplies end-to-end solutions predominantly to small to medium businesses (SMB) as well as retailors. Techrown has a wide-ranging adaptive product portfolio that is derived from market trends and is comprised of industry leading brands. Furthermore, Techrown strives to deliver more value by providing excellent after sales services for all customers, suppliers and partners alike.
